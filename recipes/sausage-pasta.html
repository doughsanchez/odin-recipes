Sausage Pasta

Description

A quick and easy recipe.

Giving a familiar recipe a new twist.

Ingredients

  • 6 sausages
  • 250g mushrooms
  • tomato sauce
  • 300g pasta
    •

Steps

  1. Remove the skins of the sausages and heat in a pan

  2. Add the mushrooms

  3. At the same time cook the pasta

  4. Mix together, and add cheese to serve
