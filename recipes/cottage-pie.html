Cottage Pie

Description

This is a warm, delicious pie made with quality ingredients

Sure to make the whole family happy and satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 750g beef mince
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 stick of celery

Steps

  1. Cook the vegetables in a large pan, until soft
  2. Add the beef mince and stir
  3. Place into a dish and cover with mashed potatoes
  4. Cook for 25 minutes in the oven
