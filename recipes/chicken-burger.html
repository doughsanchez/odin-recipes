Chicken Burger

Description

This is a delicious Sriracha and Mustard glazed chicken burger that everyone must try.

It it accompanied with a cucumber relish and a few crisp lettuce leaves.

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken thighs
  • 6 brioche burger buns
  • 3 tbsp sriracha
  • 4 tbsp american mustard
  • 6 lettuce leaves
  • 1 cucumber
  • 4 tbsp mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Flatten chicken thighs with a rolling pin, then massage with garlic, paprika, chili, ginger, and salt
  2. For the relish shred cucumber, mix with mayonnaise and lime juice
  3. Heat the grill, cook chicken for 5 mins per side, then assemble in toasted buns
